In order for this to work you need to enable experimental WASM features.
  • In Chrome enter "chrome://flags" and search for webassembly.
  • Enable Experimantal WebAssembly
  • Enable SIMD support
  • Enable threads support
  • Click Relaunch

All calculations are repeated 100 times and then averaged.
